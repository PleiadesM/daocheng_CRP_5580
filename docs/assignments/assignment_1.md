This is the first graded assignment in the course, *CRP-5580, Introduction to Web Mapping and Spatial Data Visualization*. I chose the data from the recently renewed dataset from USDA: [Farm Income and Wealth Statistics](https://www.ers.usda.gov/data-products/farm-income-and-wealth-statistics). From this dataset, I filtered the "Net farm income" among the various categories in 2024 and 2004 to make a comparison.
